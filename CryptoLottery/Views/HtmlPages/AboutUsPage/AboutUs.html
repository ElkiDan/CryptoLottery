The reason for choosing this project was to combine both fields that we study: CS and Economics, Using a very evolving and spoken on technology such as Blockchain. Our project is Crypto Lottery, More in details it's a Completly uniform decentralized lottery without a third party that can manipulate the result. The idea for the protocol was thought by Avishay yanai, And can be considered as aggregated Coin toss protocol to ensure the uniformity of the lottery.


The following is The protocol:


1. Each user generates their own secret random number N
2. Users create their commitment by hashing their N with their address: bytes32 hash = sha3(N)
note: steps 1 & 2 should be performed locally, in secret.
3. Users send their hash to the contract along a fixed amount of ether which is the bet and an additional amount that will be returned to each honest player so that it wouldn't be worth it to try and cheat.
4. Submissions continue until a sufficient participants have joined.
5. Once the submission round has ended, the reveal round begins.
6. Each user submits their random number N to the contract
7. The contract verifies that the keccak256(N) matches the original submission
8. If the user does not submit a valid N in time, his deposit is forfeit.
9. Stage 7 continues for a minimum grace period of 100 block and then each player can check if the lottery can start( The user who initiated the lottery will be rewarded by half of his bet)
10. The Ns are all XOR'd together to generate the resulting random number.
11. This number is used to determine which of the participants receives the reward. (N % numUsers)
12. All players recieve their frozen funds, The winner gets the winning prize and the Lottery initiator gets his reward.
